About Us

We help businesses with end-to-end compliance solutions, from client onboarding to real-time transaction monitoring.

Our Services

Customer Onboarding & Identity Verification

  • KYC – Fully automated identity verification
  • Document Verification – Validate passports, driver’s licenses, national IDs, etc.
  • Address Verification – Verify proof of residence using utility bills, bank statements, or geolocation data.
  • Age Verification – Ensure regulatory compliance for age-restricted services.

AML & Risk Screening

  • PEP Screening – Identify high-risk individuals linked to government or political roles.
  • Sanctions Screening – Cross-check customers against global sanctions lists.
  • Adverse Media Checks – Scan global news sources for negative mentions of customers.
  • Transaction Monitoring – Detect suspicious activities like money laundering and fraud.

Business KYC & Corporate Verification

  • Business Entity Verification – Validate company registration details and legal status.
  • UBO Identification – Identify real owners behind corporate structures.
  • Financial Statements & Credit Reports – Assess corporate financial health.

Ongoing Compliance & Regulatory Reporting

  • Continuous KYC Monitoring – Track changes in customer risk profiles in real time.
  • Regulatory Compliance Reporting – Automated reports for regulatory authorities.
  • Fraud Prevention & Identity Theft Detection – Prevent synthetic identity fraud and deepfake threats.
